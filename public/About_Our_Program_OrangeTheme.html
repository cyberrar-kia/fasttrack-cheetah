What Is FastTrack Phonics?

CHEETAH Toys & More, LLC (CHEETAH®) proudly introduces FastTrack Phonics™, a holistic reading program designed for children 5+, families, and educators. It works in homes, schools, churches, and community centers—anywhere literacy matters.

Built on decades of proven strategies, the program empowers even young learners to teach others at home. With FastTrack Phonics™, students don't just gain reading skills — they become confident leaders in literacy.

It's more than reading. It’s a movement. We are empowering individuals and building nations — one letter at a time.

Why Is There a Need for FastTrack Phonics?

  • 🌍 Globally: Over 770 million people lack basic reading skills (UNESCO, 2023).
  • 🇺🇸 USA: 37% of 4th graders read below basic level (National Institute for Literacy, 2023).
  • 🇯🇲 Jamaica: Less than 50% of Grade 4 students perform at or above literacy standards (Ministry of Education and Youth, 2023).
  • 🇺🇬 Uganda: Only 3 in 10 Primary 3 children can read a simple English story (Uwezo Uganda, 2022).
